About Us

THE PEARL is the foremost producer of the finest quality cultured pearls and a world leader in the design of exceptional jewelry. Our name is synonymous with superior quality at every stage, from the selection of finest materials to expert workmanship to our customer service commitment. Each beautiful piece reflects supreme dedication, passion and care.

The aesthetic values of Korea have been born from a way of life surrounded by the sea and a beautiful natural environment. This spirit of beauty, characterized by subtlety and elegance, has garnered attention around the world and is embodied in every piece of jewelry.
